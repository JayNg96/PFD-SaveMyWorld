About Us

Incorporated in 2021, Earthers is a learning portal that consists of articles, games, and other items for users to learn how they can play their part in saving earth.

Why Should We Protect the Environment?

When it comes to saving the environment there are many things that we can do to help. There are tons of ways that you can live a sustainable life and help to protect the environment.

So, why should we work to protect the environment and work towards a better future?

  • To Protects Humanity.
  • To Protect our Forests.
  • To Protects our Ecosystem.

So Why Should We Protect the Environment? Overall, when it comes to answering the questions why should we protect the environment, there are many reasons! This is the only place we have to live and we should want to treat it with care and respect. Make sure that you bookmark our page so you can always stay up to date with the current news!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

To find out more about us, you can either click on the box or search for frequently asked questions.

Why was the Earthers website created?

The Earther website was created to raise awareness about environmental issues and to share how one can play their part.

When was the Earthers web portal created?

The Earthers website was officially launched on 24 October 2021! Since then, we have continuously improved our website.

Where can I find Earthers' contact points and social media channels?

Scroll down to our footer below. There you can find our twitter, instagram, linkedin and contact details.

What is the purpose of this web portal?

The purpose of the website is to have a portal where environmentally conscious individuals can have a convenient platform to gain understanding of the problems that affect our world today.

What do we hope to achieve?

We hope that with this website, it will spread interests about how people from all walks of life can play a part in saving the earth no matter who they are and where they are around the world.

What are the backgrounds of the Earthers' development team?

The Earthers development team consists of students from Ngee Ann Polytechnic from courses such as Information Technology and Financial Informatics.

Must we be logged in to access all parts of Earthers?

No. Earthers has also been designed to be used as a logged-out experience. Therefore, if the user has previously logged in with his or her account with us, they will be able to access more of our resources, such as purchasing our merchandise.

Where does Earthers source its information from?

Earthers is built from information from various sources, including public, government, and commercial sources.

Does Earthers support cross-platform devices?

Yes. Earthers is compatible with most modern mobile and desktop web browsers.
