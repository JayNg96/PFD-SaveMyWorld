Play Your Part

Learn how you can play your part in saving and protecting our environment!

Here's how you can do your part!

Turn Off Unnecessary Lights

Turning off lights can help individuals save energy and money. It can also aid in the reduction of carbon emissions and other harmful greenhouse gases. As a result, turning off lights is a simple way to contribute to environmental protection and the preservation of the planet.


Use Natural Lighting

Natural lighting can naturally reduce the production of harmful organisms, and sunlight is widely regarded as one of the most effective natural disinfectants. Harnessing the power of the sun reduces carbon footprint, resulting in a positive environmental impact for future generations.
