#TeamSeas

Team Seas is an international collaborative fundraiser project run by YouTubers MrBeast and Mark Rober. They aim to raise 30 million dollars which would allow them to remove 30,000,000 pounds of marine debris from the ocean by the end of 2021.

International Jaguar Day

International Jaguar day is held on November 29. This year, Jaguar North America is collaborating with the Wildlife Conservation Society to raise awareness and support. Donate today to help!
